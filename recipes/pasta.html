Pasta carbonara

Description

Spaghetti alla carbonara. Luscious and wonderfully indulgent, pasta carbonara takes as long to make as it does to cook the pasta. The ingredients are simpleâ€”just spaghetti (or another long pasta), and the carbonara is made with pancetta or bacon, eggs, Parmesan, a little olive oil, salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 3-4 whole eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan or pecorino cheese
  • 1 pound spaghetti (or bucatini or fettuccine)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
