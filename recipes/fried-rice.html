Fried rice

Description

Fried rice is a delicious dish that will make even your children fall in love. This is also the perfect food to boost your energy, be healthy and help your body to cope with daily tasks.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white long-grain rice
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, lightly whisked /li>
  • 2 bacon rashers, chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled and grated
  • 2 shallots, trimmed, finely sliced
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Sesame seeds, to serve

Steps

  1. Cook the rice in a large saucepan of boiling water for 12 minutes or until tender. Drain and leave to cool.
  2. Heat oil in non-stick wok or large frying pan over medium heat, add eggs and cook for two minutes.
  3. Add the bacon and carrot and Stir fry 1 minute.
  4. Add shallots, peas and rice, and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add egg and soy sauce and stir until heated through.
  6. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and top with extra shallots. Serve immediately.
