Fried rice

Description

Fried rice is a delicious dish that will make even your children fall in love with it. This is also the perfect food to increase your energy, be healthy and helping your body to cope with daily tasks.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white long-grain rice
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, lightly whisked /li>
  • 2 bacon rashers, chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled and grated
  • 2 shallots, trimmed, finely sliced
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Sesame seeds, to serve
